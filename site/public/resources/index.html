LGBTQ-positive clergy and church congregations in the West Island

Cedar Park United Church à Pointe Claire

Cedar Park United is a progressive, welcoming Christian community in Pointe Claire on the West Island of Montreal that is open to all ages, races, denominations, genders and sexual orientations.

Summerlea United Church à Lachine

We are committed to building an inclusive faith community for all people regardless of age, race, ability, language, sexual orientation, gender identity or economic background.

Union United Church à Anne de Bellevue

On April 28, 2002 Union Church became the first Affirming Congregation in Quebec which means we openly welcome gay, lesbian, bisexual and transgendered persons.

Lakeshore Unitarian Universalist Congregation à Beaconsfield

Whoever you are, whatever your thoughts on spirituality, your cultural background, political leaning or sexual orientation, you are welcome in our West Island Unitarian Universalist group.
